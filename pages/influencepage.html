HOW DOES MOB INFLUENCE ME?

mob is a very compassionate child and i admire his goal of not using his powers to hurt others. he always tries his best when doing things. for example, when he joins the body improvement club, he goes all out to the point where he faints. he never resents the people around him, even those who hurt him. he just seems like someone who is always seeing the good in people. he started off as a kid that never voiced his opinions to standing up for himself against his mentor, reigen.

mob's dedication to keeping those around him protected is very admirable. he doesn't really care what position he's in, he just has such an honest and genuine desire to protect. he went through a very traumatizing and negative event that caused him to live in a fantasy world where he had no friends and everyone around his torments and bullies him. he takes away something positive out of that, saying that since the world is full of terrible people, he is lucky to have his friends and family beside him and even vowed to be more grateful towards them.
