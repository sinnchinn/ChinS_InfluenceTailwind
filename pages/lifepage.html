SHIGEO KAGEYAMA'S LIFE

SHIGEO KAGEYAMA'S LIFE

  • shigeo kageyama, nicknamed as "mob" or known as "white t poison" by other schools
  • middle schooler at salt middle school
  • socially awkward and struggles with making friends. he's also easily scared when in public
  • forgiving and understanding child, he never holds grudges or resentment even to those who hurt him
  • extremely compassionate to those around him and even enemies
  • older brother to riku kageyama
  • has an inferiority complex towards ritsu
  • reached 100% rage in front of ritsu when a couple of kids started bullying them when they were younger. ritsu has been scared of him since and has an inferiority complex toward mob
  • loves his brother
  • mob was born an esper and has been able to use extremely strong psychic powers since he was young
  • his powers are linked to his emotions. strong and negative emotions and cause him to hurt others, something he strongly avoids
  • generally tries to keep his emotions in check and bottles them up. when reaching 100% rage, it's hard for him to control his powers. however, when he reaches 100& courage, he has the honest and genuine desire to protect those around him and can control his powers
  • when fighting enemies, he refuses to use his powers unless necessary. he would rather try to talk it out with them
