SHIGEO KAGEYAMA & HIS COMPANIONS

  • scam artist, has no psychic powers but runs a business where he calls himself the greatest psychic of the 21st century
  • takes mob in as an assistant and apprentice once he figured out that mob has psychic powers
  • originally only took him in to help with his psychic business, helping customers get rid of spirits around them
  • they form a close relationship over the years, leading reigen to become too dependent on mob and begins to summon mob out of the blue for work. mob is angered by this and eventually abandons him but later reconcile
  • mob looks up to him as a mentor
  • reigen sees mob as the first friend he's made in his life
  • a self proclaimed upper class evil spirit that founded a cult called LOL. mob was lured to one of their events one day and defeated dimple with his 100% rage, assuming that he was gone for goof
  • showed up at mob's house the next day as a small green spirit
  • initially wanted ot take advantage of mob and possess him when he had the chance, but they ended up forming a friendship and dimple followed mob everywhere
  • eventually tries to make mob a god
  • sacrificed himself for mob
  • didn't know any other espers before meeting mob, greatly overestimated his powers and it shows after mob defeats him
  • self centered
  • becomes friends with mob and begins to adhere to his policies of not using his psychic powers unless necessary
  • has grown to care about people
  • considers mob as his greatest rival

    • body improvement club:

  • mob joins the body improvement club to impress his crush tsubomi after she got bored of his psychic powers
  • the club happily accepts
  • the club members are very supportive of mob and always tries to help out. for example, during his battle against teru, the club joins and tries to defeat the other schools before teru ultimately wiped them out

    • telepathy club:

  • the original club that wanted mob to join so that they can keep their room before mob joined the body improvement club
  • mob later befriends them
